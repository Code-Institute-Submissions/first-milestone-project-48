Class: Fun4Baby
When: Tuesdays
Times: 9:30 - 10:10am
Age Group: (Up to 18 months old with Parent)

Fun4Baby

Fun4Baby sessions aim to provide a framework for a baby's natural physical and psychological development. By providing regular exercise through the medium of play, baby gymnastics guides a baby's body towards a healthy development, leading to a lifetime enjoyment of physical activity.

Excerise and Socialise

If you have a baby or toddler and would like to introduce them to exercise and socialising in a safe and friendly environment, look no further than Gemini Gymnastics Fun4Baby sessions.

Our sessions aim to provide a framework for natural physical and psychological development and also encourage socialisation.

These sessions are less structured than our pre-school sessions for older children and allow parents and babies/toddlers to explore the gym and equipment at their own pace, with guidance from a trained coach.

By providing exercise from a young age through the medium of play, baby gymnastics guides a baby’s body towards healthy development provides a special time for parents and babies to enhance their relationship and leads to a lifetime enjoyment of physical activity.

At the end of the session relax and make friends with other parents in our coffee bar.

Pre-School

Pre-School sessions aim to provide children with an environment to learn and have fun - it is our aim to make it an enjoyable experience for all. Within a safe, structured and supervised environment and through the use of movement, music and apparatus, our aims are for your children to:

- Have fun in a happy and friendly atmosphere
- Develop important social, physical and educational skills
- Create opportunities to experiment
- Channel their energies in the right direction
- Provide challenges to enable them to achieve and improve
- Encourage participation in our award scheme

Class: After School - Aquamarine, Emerald, Sapphire, Ruby & Topaz
When: Classes all week
Times: See table below
Age Group: (4 1/2 year old to Adult age)

After School

We offer structured coaching in recreational, artistic and sports acro gymnastics in a safe and fun environment.

Within these sessions all children will work through the British Gymnastics Award Scheme and we aim to encourage all members, whatever their level of ability and experience, to fulfil their potential in an enjoyable but disciplined way.

Should any gymnast show potential within any of our classes, they may be invited to attend a trial into one of our competition squads.

All of our coaches are British Gymnastics trained and qualified

Please make sure...your child is in the correct class for their school year. Thank you
