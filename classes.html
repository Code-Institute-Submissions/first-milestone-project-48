Our Classes & Schedules


Class: When: Time: Age Group:
Fun4Baby Tuesdays 9:30 - 10:10am (Up to 18 months old with Parent)

Fun4Baby

Fun4Baby sessions aim to provide a framework for a baby's natural physical and psychological development. By providing regular exercise through the medium of play, baby gymnastics guides a baby's body towards a healthy development, leading to a lifetime enjoyment of physical activity.

Excerise and Socialise

If you have a baby or toddler and would like to introduce them to exercise and socialising in a safe and friendly environment, look no further than Gemini Gymnastics Fun4Baby sessions.

Our sessions aim to provide a framework for natural physical and psychological development and also encourage socialisation.

These sessions are less structured than our pre-school sessions for older children and allow parents and babies/toddlers to explore the gym and equipment at their own pace, with guidance from a trained coach.

By providing exercise from a young age through the medium of play, baby gymnastics guides a baby’s body towards healthy development provides a special time for parents and babies to enhance their relationship and leads to a lifetime enjoyment of physical activity.

At the end of the session relax and make friends with other parents in our coffee bar.
